TEDxAJCE 2021





Our theme is ‘Tracing the Roots’. Our life is filled with choices, they play a major role in shaping us as a person. Each decision we make will have an impact, big or small, on us and everything around us. This in some way contributes to shaping us and everything else into into how it is today. As we travel the journey called life, we have come across several crossroads called choices and each time we made our decision of which path to travel. Among all these choices there are some decisions that led us to key turning points in our lives, those which had a major role in making us the person we have become. When we trace back all these paths or roots, we see how they’ve shaped us into who we are today. Hence, tracing the roots is all about a retrospective into the way life is, or could have been.

“By spreading ideas worthy enough to be shared, the power of inspiration and change is created”. A global community striving towards spreading ideas from every discipline and culture, that is TED in a nutshell. It is a non-profit media organisation that helps people get the deeper meaning of ideas, attitudes, and the world itself. From its origin in 1984, TED talks have touched people’s heart. It is continued to be watched all around the world. The impact a TED event creates is unmatched. TED conferences and events inspire and motivate people towards becoming a better version of themselves. Read More Here

TEDx events are independently organized, and licensed TED-like events organized by enthusiastic communities and organizers in line with TED’s mission ‘ideas worth spreading’. Its goal is to create a unique gathering in the community that will unleash new ideas, inspire, and inform. It provides a platform where ideas are shared openly, and connections are made through conversation. It strengthens bonds among the community through discussions and promotes harmony. The event screens TED Talks videos and invites live speakers to drive ideation and start conversations. TEDx has reached a milestone of 100,000 talks and are now viewed more than 3 billion times annually. Read More Here

Established in 2017, TEDxAJCE is an independently organized TEDx event with the goal of catering the global experience of TED to our community at Amal Jyothi College of Engineering, Kanjirapally. We yearn to showcase new perspectives and ideas of speakers from all walks of life. TEDxAJCE has an enthusiastic community of students, professionals, and the community around it, open to ideas that expand their world view. Our aim is to open a window to a world of opportunities and encourage them to seek them out and pay it forward. Read More Here

Our Speakers

Henna Jayanth

A State Level Cricketer turned Race Car Driver. A model and an entrepreneur. The only woman in the Redbull Opinion Leadership Program from Kerala in motorsports. An inspiration for countless youngsters out there.

Renjith R Panathur

“I decided to fight and realise my dream," these are the words of a young man who fought all odds to reach where he is today. From working as a watchman to becoming an IIM professor, this man turned his life around purely through his hard work and dedication.

Stephen Devassy

Musician, composer, arranger and music director, this man is indeed one with many talents. Magic on his fingers and music in his heart, he will leave you elated and transport you to that mesmerizing world.

Hani Musthafa

The face of Flywheel - India’s favourite auto show. He is the only person from south India to become one among the six jurors from India for the World Car Awards. His love for wheels culminated in the creation of the Flywheel team.

Shailaja Teacher

Her impeccable approach to counter various virus surges earned global recognition and was honoured for her efforts to fight the covid pandemic by the UN and lauded the world over. She is that one person who continuous to show us to find the light even in the darkest of times.

Abeesh P Dominic

Body builder by profession, this man earned a place for him in he several world records by breaking 122 coconuts under 1 minute. He is the perfect example of how a human body can do incredible things with proper training.

VS Sabu

From making the first autonomous robot in the country to his latest creation, a Tesla coil, he has been inspiring us for more than three decades. He has shown us degrees don’t build things but passion does.
