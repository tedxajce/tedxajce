    About
    TED Events

    “By spreading ideas worthy enough to be shared, the power of inspiration and change is created”. A global community striving towards spreading ideas from every discipline and culture, that is TED in a nutshell. It is a non-profit media organisation that helps people get the deeper meaning of ideas, attitudes, and the world itself. From its origin in 1984, TED talks have touched people’s hearts. It is continued to be watched all around the world. The impact a TED event creates is unmatched. TED conferences and events inspire and motivate people towards becoming a better version of themselves.

    Read More
    TEDx Events

    TEDx events are independently organized, and licensed TED-like events organized by enthusiastic communities and organizers in line with TED’s mission ‘ideas worth spreading’. Its goal is to create a unique gathering in the community that will unleash new ideas, inspire, and inform. It provides a platform where ideas are shared openly, and connections are made through conversation. It strengthens bonds among the community through discussions and promotes harmony. The event screens TED Talks videos and invites live speakers to drive ideation and start conversations. TEDx has reached a milestone of 100,000 talks and are now viewed more than 3 billion times annually.

    Read More
    TEDx AJCE

    Aimed with the vision of catering the global experience of TED to our community at Amal Jyothi College of Engineering, Kanjirappally, TEDxAJCE is an independently organized TEDx event that came into existence in 2018 to imbibe the spirit of perceivance and inquisitiveness for all disciplines of life. TEDxAJCE has a vibrant culture of students and professionals, along with social life flourishing all around in the lap of nature. It aims to widen perspectives by welcoming the light of knowledge from all around the world, thus strengthening and empowering the community with incredible ideas from passionate minds.
